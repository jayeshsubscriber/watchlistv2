# Watchlist Signals – UX options

The watchlist currently uses **Option A** for showing signals on stocks.

---

## Option A (Current): Inline tags below scrip

- **What:** Small pill tags (e.g. "Dividend", "News", "O=H") in a wrap row directly under the scrip name and exchange.
- **Pros:** Visible at a glance, no extra tap; works in multi-column layout.
- **Cons:** Can add height when many signals; may need truncation or “+N” for very long lists.

---

## Option B: Signal count badge + expand

- **What:** A small badge (e.g. `3`) or icon next to the scrip; tap row or badge to expand and show signal list in a card below.
- **Pros:** Compact rows; detail on demand.
- **Cons:** One extra tap to see what the signals are.

---

## Option C: Dedicated “Signals” column

- **What:** In multi-column view, add a “Signals” column showing abbreviated tags or icons (e.g. D=Dividend, N=News, V=Volume Spike).
- **Pros:** Aligned layout; easy to scan down the column.
- **Cons:** Uses horizontal space; abbreviations need a legend or tooltips.

---

## Option D: Bottom sheet / detail on tap

- **What:** Tap a row to open a bottom sheet or detail view with full signal list and short descriptions.
- **Pros:** Room for more context and actions.
- **Cons:** Signals not visible in the list itself.

---

To try another option, say which one (B, C, or D) and we can adapt the UI accordingly.
